Spaghetti

Description

A classic Italian favorite, this dish features al dente spaghetti topped with juicy homemade meatballs and a rich tomato sauce, finished with a sprinkle of freshly grated Parmesan cheese. Perfect for a hearty family dinner or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic, herbs
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Mix and form meatballs using ground beef, garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brown the meatballs in a pan with olive oil.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes to the pan and simmer with the meatballs.
  5. Drain spaghetti and plate it.
  6. Top with meatballs and sauce, then sprinkle with Parmesan.
