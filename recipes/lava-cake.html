Lava Cake

Description

This decadent chocolate lava cake features a warm, gooey center that flows out with every bite. Topped with powdered sugar, fresh strawberries, and a sprig of mint, it’s an elegant dessert perfect for special occasions or indulgent nights in.

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Strawberries and Powdered Sugar (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together until smooth.
  2. Whisk eggs and sugar, then combine with the chocolate mixture.
  3. Fold in flour gently until just mixed.
  4. Pour into ramekins and bake until edges are set but center is molten.
  5. Invert and serve warm, garnished with strawberries, mint, and powdered sugar.
  6. Serve hot, add vanilla ice cream if you prefer à la mode.
